Who We Are

The story behind Munyax Eco

Munyax Eco is a Rwandan clean energy company on a mission to accelerate Africa's energy transition. Founded in 2018, we design, install, and maintain solar energy solutions for households, businesses, and institutions across East Africa.

We believe clean energy is a right, not a privilege. With a team that is 60% women, we are proving that sustainability and inclusion are two sides of the same coin, powering progress while protecting our planet.

01

Our Mission

Catalyze the energy transition across Africa by delivering clean, affordable, and smart energy solutions with a commitment to the active participation of women and youth.

02

Our Vision

A future where every East African community has access to reliable, sustainable, and affordable clean energy, driving economic growth and environmental stewardship.
